## The 3-tier keyword strategy

Target the keyword universe in three waves based on KD (keyword difficulty) and time-to-rank:

| Tier | KD range | When to target | Goal |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1** | 1-20 | Weeks 1-8 | Quick wins, fast rankings, build domain authority |
| **Tier 2** | 21-50 | Weeks 9-16 | Category terms, medium-term moat |
| **Tier 3** | 51+ | Month 4+ (DR 30+) | Category leadership flagships |

## Keyword research tools + workflow

### Tools to use
- **Ahrefs** (primary) — keyword gap, SERP analysis, competitor tracking
- **SEMrush** (secondary) — for secondary keyword ideas
- **Google Keyword Planner** — for commercial intent validation
- **AlsoAsked** — for PAA expansion
- **Glimpse / Exploding Topics** — for trending sub-niches (e.g., new AI engines as they launch)

### Weekly keyword research ritual (30 min, Mondays)
1. Check Ahrefs Organic Keywords for AnswerMonk — new ranking keywords?
2. Check Content Gap vs Profound/Otterly/Peec — new displacement opportunities?
3. Run Keywords Explorer on 3 seed terms: `aeo [month]`, `ai visibility [year]`, plus 1 from recent news
4. Run 20-query SERP audit — AI Overview presence, competitor positions
5. Add any new keywords with volume > 100 AND KD < 30 to the backlog

### Monthly keyword audit (1 hour, first Monday)
- Review all ranking keywords
- Identify keywords ranked 4-20 (boost to page 1 candidates)
- Identify keywords that dropped (diagnose + refresh content)
- Update the 200-query canonical tracking list (swap out 20 stale, add 20 new)

## Decision rules

### When to target a new keyword
Must pass all:
- Volume > 100/month (except branded competitor keywords)
- KD < 30 (at DR 0) or KD < 50 (at DR 20+)
- Intent matches our product or positioning
- Not on kill list
- Not already targeted by existing page

### When to publish vs ignore
- Ship a dedicated page if: volume > 500 OR intent is bottom-funnel
- Ship an FAQ/section within existing page if: volume < 500 AND related to existing topic
- Ignore if: volume < 100 OR kill list OR KD > 70 AT DR 0

### When to double down on a keyword
- Ranking moved into top 20 within 60 days → add more internal links + refresh
- Ranking at #4-10 → add first-party data, expand content to claim #1
- High CTR but low conversion → add product-relevant CTA mid-article

### When to kill a keyword target
- 12 weeks post-publish, ranking still outside top 50 AND no LLM citations → reassign URL to different keyword
- Google manually penalized content → remove/redirect
- Category pivoted (e.g., if "AEO" term fades in favor of "LLMO") → pivot content accordingly
