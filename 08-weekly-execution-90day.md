## Task distribution per week (typical)

- LinkedIn content + engagement: 25%
- Customer development + feedback: 20%
- Distribution (DMs, directories, PR): 15%

## What NOT to do per week (explicit kill list)

### Don't do in Weeks 1-4:
- ❌ Launch paid product (product isn't ready, audience isn't built)
- ❌ Paid ads (no clear conversion path yet)
- ❌ Build partnership integrations (too early)
- ❌ Hire sales team (no enterprise prospects)

### Don't do in Weeks 5-8:
- ❌ Skip weekly measurement ritual
- ❌ Miss LinkedIn cadence (3-5 posts/week)
- ❌ Expand to new channels (master LinkedIn first)
- ❌ Build new features not requested by customers

### Don't do in Weeks 9-12:
- ❌ Stop content cadence (momentum drops fast)
- ❌ Ignore study-driven press cycle
- ❌ Build features not requested by enterprise prospects
- ❌ Scale team before 100 paying customers

## Weekly decision checkpoints

Every Friday, apply these rules:

**If founder LinkedIn < 3 posts this week:**
→ Halt new content production. Fix distribution first.

**If < 1 canonical page published this week (Weeks 1-6):**
→ Fix prioritization. Content is the moat.

**If < 1 LLM citation added this week (Week 4+):**
→ Audit content against `09-blog-rules.md`.

**If paying customers < targeted for that week:**
→ Week 6+: Fix pricing / trial flow. Week 9+: Fix enterprise conversion.

**If founder hours > 60/week consistently:**
→ Hiring gap. Add content writer or marketing ops.

---

## Contingency plans

### If flagship study launch underperforms (Week 10)
- Amplify through influencer outreach (Week 11)
- Create derivative content (Weeks 11-12)
- Re-launch in 6 weeks with refreshed angle

### If paid product conversion < 5% (Week 6-8)
- Pricing test (lower starter to $99?)
- Simplify trial onboarding
- Delay enterprise tier launch

### If indexing issues persist (Week 2-3)
- Direct submission via GSC URL Inspection
- Check robots.txt for accidental blocking
- Verify sitemap.xml submission
- Cross-link from high-authority pages

### If competitor ships major release (any week)
- Rapid-response content piece within 72 hours
- Accelerate complementary feature development
- Don't pivot strategy — iterate within existing moat

---

## The weekly non-negotiables

Every week, minimum must-haves:

- ✅ Monday: Analysis ritual complete
- ✅ Tuesday-Thursday: At least 1 canonical page published
- ✅ Friday: Measurement + stakeholder report
- ✅ Founder LinkedIn: 3+ posts
- ✅ Customer conversations: 2-3 user interviews or feedback calls
- ✅ Backlog review: Sprint priorities set for next week

If ANY of these miss 2 weeks in a row → systemic problem. Diagnose and fix.
